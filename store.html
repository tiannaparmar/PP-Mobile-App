Dietary Restrictions


At Save-A-Waste your satisfaction and saftey Is very valuable to us therefore, we have ensured to accomodate dietery restriction symbols. E.g. vegetarian, milk product, nuts, gluten etc. Beside each of the food items that are sold on our app.

Kindly, make yourself aware of these symbols, to avoid any inconveiniences. Please note, that Save-A-Waste will not be held responsible in the event of any medical accident caused by the food items and their contents after the purchase has been made.
